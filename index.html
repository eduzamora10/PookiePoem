A Poem To Pookie...

Roses are red, violets are blue.

My love for you is pure and true.

With every kiss and every cuddle,

My heart beats rapidly with no struggle.

You are the light in my darkest days,

the one who brightens all my ways.

Your presence fills my heart with joy,

Your value to me is like a child's favorite toy.

Your kindness is unlimited, your presence is bright.

With every moment I am with you, everything just feels right.

You are the protagonist of my life, my very own star.

Without your existence, I will always be subpar.

I am grateful for you who I love the most.

Your love is a gift I treasure and boast.

I will cherish you for all my days.

Our love for each other, will forever be an endless phase.
